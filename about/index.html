As iron sharpens iron, so one man sharpens another.

Our Mission

Men of God exists to equip men to lead with courage, conviction, and Christ at the center. We believe men are called to stand firm, build strong families, and serve their communities with humility and strength.

Our ministry is built on a simple truth:

God uses ordinary men — from every background — to do extraordinary things.

Our Story

We’re two men from two different worlds — different careers, different upbringings — yet God brought our families together and made us brothers. One of us spent 24 years traveling the world in the United States Air Force. The other has lived his entire life in Georgia, working hard, raising a family, and serving faithfully in his church.

  • Our families are united.

  • Our values are united.

  • Our mission is united.

We’re here to encourage men to lead boldly, love deeply, and walk in the strength God provides.

Our Hosts

Tom

Retired Air Force veteran, husband, father, and co‑host of Men of God.

A husband and father of five, raising three sons and two daughters. Renewed in his Christian faith, he strives to lead with honesty, humility, and intention. Through his daily choices, he works to set an example for his family and encourage other men to grow as leaders under God. As a husband, father, and mentor, he brings a bold, battle‑tested perspective to Men of God — calling men to courage, discipline, and unwavering faith.

DeShun

Husband, father, and co‑host and founder of Men of God.

A devoted husband and father of five, raising four sons and a daughter. He and his family are active in their church, and his faith guides how he leads at home and serves his community. Grounded in God’s truth, he seeks to live with integrity, humility, and purpose, modeling what it means to lead a family under God. As a husband, father, and mentor, he brings a bold, battle‑tested perspective to Men of God — calling men to courage, discipline, and unwavering faith.
